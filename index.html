School misogyny surge 'prompted by Andrew Tate'

19 September 2025

Paul Faulkner
Local Democracy Reporting Service

Share ‚Ü± Save ‚òä

The influence of self-proclaimed misogynist Andrew Tate sparked "a surge" in similar attitudes in secondary schools in South Ribble and had to be tackled with targeted action, a report said.

Local councillors have been exploring the degree to which misogyny was a problem in the area, following concerns from teachers and parents. The report highlights that the issue is not isolated to one school.
